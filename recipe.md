# POKE BOWL
## INGREDIENTS 
 
* 200 g cooked brown rice or quinoa
* 70 g Tempeh/Tofu or mushrooms
* ½ a small red chilli
* 1 small clove garlic
* Small piece fresh ginger
* 2 scallions/ spring onions
* 1 tbsp Tamari
* 35 g frozen edamame beans or peas
* 1 small carrot
* 1 ripe avocado
* ½ a fresh mango
* Garnish;
* Toasted Sesame seeds
* 1 limes or ½ a lemon
